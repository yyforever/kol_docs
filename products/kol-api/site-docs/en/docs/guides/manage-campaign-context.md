# Manage Campaign Context

As your team uses NoxInfluencer more often, continuity matters more than one more fresh search.

## Why context matters

- It reduces repeated setup work
- It preserves shortlist and monitoring decisions
- It makes cross-session workflows more reliable

## What you should keep consistent

- Your target market, category, and brand requirements
- The shortlist you have already reviewed
- The creators you want to keep monitoring
- The creators you have already ruled out, and why

## Current status

We are still expanding campaign management. If you need a stable workflow today, start with discovery, analysis, and monitoring, and keep your campaign notes and decisions consistent across sessions.
