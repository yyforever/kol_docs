# Evaluate Creators Before Outreach

Many teams lose time not because they cannot find creators, but because they move into outreach too early.

## Key questions

- Is this creator worth more time and budget?
- Does the audience match the target market?
- Are there clear signs of risk or instability?

## Recommended outcomes

- Continue toward contact or monitoring
- Keep the creator under observation
- Exclude the creator early
