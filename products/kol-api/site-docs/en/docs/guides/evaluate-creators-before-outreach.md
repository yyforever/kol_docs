# Evaluate Creators Before Outreach

Many teams lose time not because they cannot find creators, but because they move into outreach too early.

## Key questions

- Is this creator worth more time and budget?
- Does the audience match the target market?
- Are there clear signs of risk or instability?

## Suggested evaluation order

1. Review overall credibility and abnormal signals first
2. Confirm whether the audience matches the target market
3. Decide whether the creator should move into contact or monitoring

## When not to move straight into outreach

- You have not confirmed audience fit yet
- Performance signals do not support the collaboration expectation
- There are visible controversy, fraud, or stability concerns

## Recommended outcomes

- Continue toward contact or monitoring
- Keep the creator under observation
- Exclude the creator early
