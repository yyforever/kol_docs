# Influencer Marketing with AI Agents

The most immediate value of AI agents in influencer marketing is not full automation. It is compressing the highest-friction parts of the workflow.

## The parts most worth compressing

- finding candidates
- deciding who deserves more effort
- continuing observation after the first decision
