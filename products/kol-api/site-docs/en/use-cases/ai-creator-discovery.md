# AI Creator Discovery

The first value most teams feel is not negotiation. It is getting to a credible shortlist faster.

## Traditional problem

- Discovery inputs are scattered
- Search results are often too broad
- Teams spend time before they even know who is worth evaluating

## Why this matters

Creator discovery is the step that decides whether the rest of the workflow can move quickly.
